
# Layman's Prompt Pack for Retail LLM Training  
### (Simple, easy-to-understand prompts for common users)

These prompts are written in very simple, everyday language so any layman can use your LLM easily.  
Tone required: **Simple • Friendly • Gentle • Supportive • Easy for anyone to understand**

---

# 1. Simple Business Questions
- "Explain my business performance in very simple words."
- "Tell me what is going good and what needs improvement in my shop."
- "Help me understand my sales in an easy way."
- "Explain my stock levels as if you are teaching a beginner."
- "Tell me my top-selling items in simple language."

---

# 2. Easy Planning Prompts
- "Make a simple plan for increasing my sales."
- "Tell me how I should plan my stock for next month."
- "Explain how much inventory I should keep in easy steps."
- "Help me understand what to buy more and what to buy less."

---

# 3. Simple HR & Staff Prompts
- "Tell me how many staff I need in an easy way."
- "Explain the attendance or salary issues in simple words."
- "Help me make a simple staff duty plan."

---

# 4. Customer-Friendly Prompts
- "Explain who my customers are in very simple language."
- "Tell me what type of customers buy the most from my shop."
- "Help me understand how to attract more customers."

---

# 5. Vendor / Supplier Prompts
- "Explain which suppliers are good and which are not, in simple words."
- "Tell me how I can manage my suppliers easily."

---

# 6. Store Operations (Layman Style)
- "Explain my store's daily work in an easy way."
- "Tell me what should be done every morning and evening in the store."
- "Help me understand which products need more attention."

---

# 7. Finance & Budget (Very Simple)
- "Explain my profit and loss in very easy language."
- "Tell me how much money I am earning vs spending."
- "Help me plan a basic budget for next month."

---

# 8. Simple Forecasting Prompts
- "Tell me in simple words what items will sell more next month."
- "Explain upcoming festivals and how they will affect sales."
- "Help me plan based on weather in easy words."

---

# 9. Warehouse & Supply Prompts
- "Explain where the delay is happening in simple words."
- "Tell me which products are stuck in the warehouse."
- "Help me understand if my deliveries are fast or slow."

---

# 10. Universal Layman Prompt
“Explain everything to me in very simple words. No technical terms.  
Tell me what is happening, why it is happening, and what I should do next.”
