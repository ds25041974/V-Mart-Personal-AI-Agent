# SECTION 1 — EXPERT PROMPTS (Full Detail)

## Strategy & OKRs
- Create full strategy decks with vision, mission, OKRs, KPIs, risks, governance.  
- Generate annual objectives with clear ownership and milestones.

## BI & MIS
- Build dashboard frameworks, metrics dictionaries, automation rules.  
- Create daily/weekly/monthly MIS formats.

## Merchandising
- Range planning, size sets, OTB, GMROI optimisation, assortment depth/width.  
- Markdown, liquidation, pricing ladder.

## Forecasting
- Weather-based forecasting, festive uplift, demand curves.  
- 30/60/90 day projections and trend cycles.

## Inventory
- Stock cover, ageing, replenishment, DOH, OOS risk.  
- Allocation algorithms + IST balancing engine.

## Operations
- Productivity dashboards, SOPs, audit checklists, execution frameworks.

## HR & Payroll
- Workforce planning, roster optimisation, cost-to-sales, productivity.

## Finance
- Budget sheets, GMROI improvement levers, ROI modelling.

## Supply Chain
- Warehouse capacity modelling, transport optimisation, TAT governance.

## Customer & Vendor
- Customer lifecycle segmentation, repeat % analysis, CLV inputs.  
- Vendor scorecards (OTIF, fill rate, ageing, quality, margin).

## PMO
- Project governance, RAG reviews, workflows, automation opportunities.

## VM
- VM storyboards, planograms, seasonal themes, layout rules.

## Retail Algorithms
- SQL logic & pseudocode for: allocation, IST, markdown, forecasting, alerts.

## Exception Alerts
- Sales drop, margin dip, ageing alerts, HR anomalies, supply chain delays.

# SECTION 2 — LAYMAN PROMPTS (For normal retail users)

## Business Understanding
- “Explain my business performance in simple words.”  
- “What is doing well and what needs improvement?”

## Inventory & Stock
- “Tell me which items are selling fast or slow.”  
- “Explain my stock condition simply.”

## Staff & HR
- “How many staff do I need?”  
- “Explain attendance and salary issues in easy language.”

## Customers
- “Who are my main customers?”  
- “What can I do to bring more customers?”

## Operations
- “Tell me what to check in my store every day.”  
- “Explain what products need attention.”

## Finance
- “Explain my profit and loss simply.”  
- “Tell me if I am earning or losing money.”

## Forecasting
- “Which items will sell more next month?”  
- “Explain festive or weather impact on sales.”

## Supply Chain
- “Explain which items are delayed.”  
- “Tell me if my deliveries are fast or slow.”

## Universal Layman Prompt
- “Explain everything in very simple words and tell me what I should do next.”
